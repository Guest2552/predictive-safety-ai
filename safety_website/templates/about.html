About the Project

Our Technology

Goal: The goal is to build a model that can predict the severity of future incidents (Low, Medium, High, Critical) based on textual incident reports, enabling organizations to respond more effectively.

Methodology

Text Preprocessing: Incident reports are tokenized using a specialized DistilBERT tokenizer from the Hugging Face library, which is optimized for Transformer models.

Feature Engineering: The tokenizer converts raw text into numerical token embeddings, capturing deep semantic meaning rather than just word frequency (like TF-IDF).

Model Selection: A DistilBERT Transformer model was fine-tuned for this specific multi-class text classification task, providing state-of-the-art performance.

Dual Analysis: The app provides two outputs: (1) A quantitative risk classification from the fine-tuned Transformer and (2) A qualitative analysis from the Gemini 2.0 Flash LLM.
